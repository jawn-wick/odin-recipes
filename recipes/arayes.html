Meat Stuffed Pita (Arayes)

A deliciously simple weeknight meal

Authentic Palestinian street food that is sure to impress housemates and guests alike.

Arayes are Middle Eastern sandwiches traditionally made with pita bread stuffed with spiced mixture of ground beef or lamb.

Ingredients

  • 3 Pita breads
  • 500g Minced meat (I use 50:50 lamb & beef mince with 20-30% fat)
  • 30g Parsley
  • 1 Medium brown onion
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2tbsp Pomegranate Molasses
  • 1-2tbsp Olive oil
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 1/2tsp Baharat (Middle Eastern spice mix)
  • 1/4tsp Pepper
  • 1/4tsp Cayenne
  • 1/8tsp Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Finely chop vegetables by hand or with a food processor.
  2. Add mixture to the minced meat along with spices and molasses.
  3. Slice pitas into halves or quarters and fill with spiced meat.
  4. Brush a small amount of olive oil on each side of pita.
  5. Pan fry at medium heat until crispy or oven bake at 200C for 8-10 minutes per side
