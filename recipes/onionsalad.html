Onion Sumac Salad

Greater than the sum of its parts

This delicious salad is crunchy, tangy and very versatile. The perfect accompaniment to Middle Eastern meat and chicken dishes.

Ingredients

  • Red Onion
  • Salt
  • Sumac
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Finely slice red onions and roughly chop parsley.
  2. Massage onions with salt and sumac.
  3. After 10 minutes mix the parsley and onions together.
